NEW PODIUM
IN MIRABAD AVENUE

On March 1, PODIUM turns a new page in the development and culture of luxury shopping in the capital Tashkent, by introducing a new fashion space and a new logo.

By launching its first boutique in the Mirabad Avenue fashion alley project, the PODIUM team is taking another evolutionary step in the luxury sector in the New Uzbekistan.

Mirabad Avenue is the first and only world-class luxury area in Tashkent, featuring the largest concentration of luxury, and is comparable to such world-famous fashion locations as Fifth Avenue in New York, Via Monte Napoleone in Milan, Avenue Montaigne in Paris, or Bond Street in London.

On March 25, PODIUM organized Contemporary fashion show in Tashkent.

The synergy of art, architecture and fashion within these walls of the Center for Contemporary Art, built in the industrial style in 1912, is the pearl of Tashkent architecture.

This historical location became the inspiration for the conceptual Fashion Show, defining its format and mood.Director-stylist of the show: Daniil Berg.

In this form, the building of the Center for Contemporary Art will exist for a very short time - in the near future, the space is waiting for a large-scale restoration. Guests had a unique opportunity to enjoy the pristine architecture and conceptual Fashion Show.
